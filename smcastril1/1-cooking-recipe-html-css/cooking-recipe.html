Kiwi Jam

Ingredients:

  • 1Kg of Kiwis (preferably ripe)
  • 750g of sugar

Step By Step:

Peel and chop the kiwis into small pieces or slices,
then place them into a bowl. In order to add the sugar later.
Stir well until everything is mixed.
Transfer the mixture into a pot and bring it to a boil over high heat.
Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to medium-low
and simmer for about 30 minutes to 45 minutes.
To check if the jam is ready, perform the cold plate test:
take a few drops of jam from the pot and place them on a cold plate.
If it thickens, it's ready.
Sterilize some jars beforehand and store the jam in them

Did you know...?

  1. Place a tea towel folded four times on the bottom
    of a pressure cooker. Fill the cooker to about three fingers of height
    from the bottom, depending on the size of the jar are using. Place it
    on the heat and wait for it to start boiling.
  2. Fill the jars (clean and sterilized) with the jam you've prepared, leaving
    about one finger of space from the top of the edge. Seal the jars and and
    introduce them into a pot and individually wrap them with a cloth to prevent
    them from breaking when they come into contact with each other.
  3. Close the pot, and when steam begins to escape, let it cook
    for 20 to 25 minutes (this time is suitable for three medium-sized jars).
    Then, turn off the heat and allow all the steam to escape through the valve.
  4. Remove the jars and let them cool upside down at room temperature for 24 HOURS.
